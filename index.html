About Charlie Coin

Charlie Coin is a meme token created to honor the legacy of a silent film legend. Inspired by the artistry and timeless appeal of classic cinema, Charlie Coin aims to bring together enthusiasts and investors who appreciate both humor and history in the world of digital currency.
